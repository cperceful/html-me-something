The Dogs In My Life

  • Dax
  • Blue
  • Sadie

So, I love dogs. Like, a lot. Now that I don't live with my parents, I technically don't have one of my own. But I think of all of their dogs as my dogs too. Let's talk about them

Dax

She's 8 years old now, almost 9. She's very smart, even though she's had no training of any kind. Use a word with her 3 or 4 times and she understands it. She's my dad's dog, and he spoils her rotten. Also, she's really fat

Blue

He's just about 3 now. He's...not the smartest. He likes to jump on you and lick your face. He loves laser pointers too much. He is my mom and stepdad's dog

Sadie

She's almost 10 years old now, and she's a grumpy old lady. She moves a little slower than she used to, and plays a little less. But she'll always be my favorite person. She's technically my mom and stepdad's dog, but everybody agrees that at least emotionally, she's my dog.

Honorable mention: Dany

But Dany was found in a parking lot by my mom when she was way too young to be seperated from her litter. She's basically been raised by Sadie and Blue. In a lot of ways, she acts more like a dog than a cat. She's pretty cool
